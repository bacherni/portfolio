# Portfolio of Work – Niklas Bacher

This portfolio presents selected academic and analytical projects that reflect my research interests in 
computational social science, spatial resilience and complex systems modeling.  

---

### 1. [Bachelor’s Thesis – From the Capital to the Periphery: The Impact of State Presence in Shaping Conflict Dynamics in Africa](./bachelors-thesis)
Empirical study using PRIO-Grid data and neural networks to analyze how local infrastructure shapes conflict dynamics.  
**Methods:** R, Python, spatial econometrics, multinomial logistic regression  
**Keywords:** conflict prediction, infrastructure, geospatial data

---

### 2. [Term Paper – Moral-Emotional Language on Reddit](./moral-language-replication)
Replication of Brady et al. (2017) on moral contagion using Reddit data.  
**Methods:** Python, NLP, sentiment analysis, negative binomial models  
**Keywords:** moral language, diffusion, online behavior

---

### 3. [Research Project – Disease Outbreaks, State Capacity & Protests](./disease-protest-dynamics)
Integration of zoonotic disease and protest data into a spatially disaggregated database.  
**Methods:** SQL, R, spatial panel data  
**Keywords:** political instability, public health, complex systems
